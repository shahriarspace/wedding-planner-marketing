A wedding checklist is your roadmap to a stress-free celebration. Whether you're planning a 12-month engagement or a 6-month sprint, this month-by-month guide covers every task you need to complete before the big day.

## 12+ Months Before the Wedding

The early stages are all about big decisions and setting the foundation:

- [ ] Set your overall wedding budget
- [ ] Choose your wedding date (or narrow to 2-3 options)
- [ ] Decide on your wedding style and vision
- [ ] Create your guest list draft
- [ ] Research and book your venue
- [ ] Start researching photographers and videographers
- [ ] Choose your wedding party (bridesmaids, groomsmen, etc.)
- [ ] Start a wedding planning app or binder

> **Tip**: Venues and top photographers book 12-18 months in advance. Don't delay on these!

## 9-12 Months Before

Time to lock in your major vendors:

- [ ] Book your photographer and videographer
- [ ] Book your caterer or confirm venue catering
- [ ] Book your DJ or band
- [ ] Book your officiant
- [ ] Start dress shopping (allow 6-8 months for ordering and alterations)
- [ ] Research and book a florist
- [ ] Set up your wedding website
- [ ] Plan your engagement party (optional)
- [ ] Start thinking about honeymoon destinations

## 6-9 Months Before

The middle phase is about filling in the details:

- [ ] Send save-the-dates
- [ ] Finalize your guest list
- [ ] Register for gifts
- [ ] Book hotel room blocks for out-of-town guests
- [ ] Plan your rehearsal dinner
- [ ] Choose and order bridesmaid dresses
- [ ] Book transportation (limo, shuttle, etc.)
- [ ] Begin planning your honeymoon
- [ ] Schedule hair and makeup trials
- [ ] Order your wedding cake or desserts
- [ ] Plan your ceremony details (readings, music, vows)

## 3-6 Months Before

Things start to get real:

- [ ] Send wedding invitations (aim for 8-10 weeks before)
- [ ] Order wedding rings
- [ ] Plan your seating chart (start the draft)
- [ ] Finalize your ceremony details and timeline
- [ ] Book honeymoon travel and accommodations
- [ ] Schedule final dress fitting
- [ ] Plan bachelor/bachelorette parties
- [ ] Create your wedding day timeline
- [ ] Choose your music playlist / first dance song
- [ ] Order wedding favors
- [ ] Purchase gifts for wedding party
- [ ] Apply for your marriage license

## 1-3 Months Before

Final confirmations and last details:

- [ ] Confirm all vendor details and timelines
- [ ] Finalize seating chart
- [ ] Create your photo shot list
- [ ] Write your vows (if doing personal vows)
- [ ] Plan your day-of emergency kit
- [ ] Break in your wedding shoes
- [ ] Final dress fitting
- [ ] Confirm rehearsal dinner details
- [ ] Send rehearsal dinner invitations
- [ ] Prepare wedding party gifts
- [ ] Create a packing list for the wedding day and honeymoon
- [ ] Arrange for someone to handle gifts and cards on the day

## 1-2 Weeks Before

The home stretch:

- [ ] Confirm final guest count with caterer
- [ ] Give final timeline to wedding party and vendors
- [ ] Pick up your wedding dress
- [ ] Prepare vendor payments and tips
- [ ] Delegate day-of responsibilities
- [ ] Practice your first dance
- [ ] Get your marriage license
- [ ] Prepare your vows (final version)
- [ ] Pack for the honeymoon

## Wedding Week

- [ ] Attend rehearsal and rehearsal dinner
- [ ] Hand off emergency kit, timeline, and vendor contacts to your day-of coordinator
- [ ] Get a good night's sleep
- [ ] Eat breakfast on the wedding day
- [ ] Enjoy every moment!

## After the Wedding

Don't forget these post-wedding tasks:

- [ ] Send thank-you notes (within 3 months)
- [ ] Change your name (if applicable)
- [ ] Preserve your wedding dress
- [ ] Order your wedding album
- [ ] Review vendors online
- [ ] Return any rented items
